About Nevents

Nevents is your gateway to a world of exciting events, bringing together the alumni community of African Leadership University. We are dedicated to fostering connections, personal and professional growth, and cherished memories among our valued alumni.

Our Mission

At Nevents, our mission is to provide a platform that enables alumni to reconnect, learn, and thrive. We believe in the power of shared experiences and knowledge to create lasting impact.

Our Team

The Nevents team is composed of passionate and dedicated individuals who work tirelessly to curate and organize meaningful events. We have a diverse group of event planners, web developers, and alumni engagement experts. Together, we strive to make each event a memorable experience for you.
